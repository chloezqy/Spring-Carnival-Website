Theme Logo Designer


Each fall, Spring Carnival Committee selects a new theme for the following Spring Carnival. The logo is then designed by one of the CMU students. Meet our logo designer for Spring Carnival 2024 theme Arcade!




Airla Fan


Airla is a sophomore studying Architecture and Human-Computer Interaction, and she's originally from Hong Kong. She really likes designing for various campus organizations because she find that it keeps her stimulated with a constant stream of new creative challenges and opportunities. In her free time, she loves to sing, write and continue dabbling in different creative ventures. She's very happy to have been given this opportunity to be the theme designer for Spring Carnival this year and to be included in this amazing tradition!
