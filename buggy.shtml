Buggy, also known as Sweepstakes, is a competition where Greek and independent organizations race with their buggies, small, low, aerodynamic vehicles, powered only by gravity and human pushers. At its fastest, a buggy can reach speeds up to 35 miles per hour. And yes - there's a girl in there! For more about Buggy and the complete schedule, check out the Buggy website.

Buggy Sweepstakes has been held on or behind campus every year since 1920!

You can catch the Sweepstakes Races on Friday and Saturday mornings, with Friday morning being the Preliminary Sweepstake Races from 8am to noon, the Exhibition Races at 8am on Saturday, and the Sweepstakes Final Races from 9am to noon on Saturday!
