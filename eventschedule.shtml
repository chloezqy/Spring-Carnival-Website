Event Schedule


We have so many fun events planned for Spring Carnival 2024! Check out the full schedule for more.


Thursday, April 3rd:


Midway Opening Ceremony

3:00pm - 3:30pm @ Pavillion

Join us for the official opening ceremony for Midway as we unveil the newly-completed booths! Our students have worked day and night, implementing technology and creativity to build an amazing gallery of talent!


Kiltie Band Performance

3:30pm - 4:15pm @ Kirr Commons

Carnegie Mellon's own Kiltie Band consists of musicians and colorguard and will be playing after our opening ceremony!


Activities Tent

3:30pm - 7:00pm @ The Cut

Join Spring Carnival for a variety of fun activities at various tents at the center of CMU's greens!


Scavenger Hunt Kickoff

4:00pm @ Activities Tent

Join us on a 24-hour Scavenger Hunt starting at the Activities Tent on Thursday! Form a team of 1-4 and compete to snap photos of various challenges across campus. Submit your finds via our Google Form to win exciting prizes like Squishmellows, Lego sets, and more. If you can, join us at the Activities Tent Thursday 4pm and Saturday noon for brief opening and prize ceremonies. Everyone gets a Participation Prize (while supplies last)! Don't miss out—join the adventure!


C# Performance

5:00pm - 7:00pm @ Legacy Plaza

Get excited for CMU's largest singing organization to perform a variety of accapella numbers!


Tartans Got Talent

7:00pm - 9:00pm @ Legacy Plaza

Prepare to be enthralled by a mesmerizing array of performances, including beatboxing, soul-stirring vocals, classical melodies, and dynamic dance routines. Join us for an unforgettable evening and witness the magic as these talented Tartans showcase their skills. Don't miss out on this extraordinary showcase of talent happening on Thursday 7-9pm in McConomy!


Friday, April 4th:


Activities Tent

11:00am - 7:00pm @ The Cut

Join Spring Carnival for a variety of fun activities at various tents at the center of CMU's greens!


Accapella Show

11:00am - 1:00pm @ Kirr Commons

Get ready to be blown away by CMU's finest vocal talents at our annual A Cappella Showcase. Join us on Friday, April 4th, from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM in Kirr Commons for an unforgettable performance featuring renowned singing groups: Soundbytes, Counterpoint, Joyful Noise, Treblemakers, Saans, and Originals.


CMU Swing

2:00pm @ Kirr Commons

Come out and meet CMU's very own brass quintet playing a selection of favorites, such as Les Mis, Dream a Little Dream of Me, and others!


Dance Show

2:30pm - 4:00pm @ Wiegand

Get ready to groove at our Dance Showcase! Join us on Friday, April 4th, from 2:30 to 4:00 PM in Wiegand for a dynamic display of talent featuring CMU's top dance groups. Our lineup includes Ballroom Dance Club, CMU Street Styles, Tartan Salsa, CMU KPDC, and En Pointe. With a variety of styles and mesmerizing routines, this event promises to captivate audiences of all ages.

Activities Tent
